About NUCIIE

What We Do

NU-CIIE works to provide a platform to its students, so that may share their ideas, innovations and concepts. It conducts training programmes in the field of entrepreneurial skill development and develops entrepreneurship related course curriculum to incorporate in management and engineering programmes. It also provides guidance and facilities to first time entrepreneurs during gestation.

Vision

CIIE views to build an Entrepreneurial Ecosystem to provide a platform for students with creative minds and ideas and the potential to create business opportunities.

Overview

The Centre for Innovation Incubation and Entrepreneurship (CIIE) was started to encourage participation in the field of entrepreneurship and innovation. It works to promote the sharing of new ideas between ingenious minds and gives them a platform to collaborate.

Mission

CIIE aims to create awareness about Entrepreneurship and develop such skills among the students. It intends to nurture the budding entrepreneurs within NU and provide them with equalopportunities.
